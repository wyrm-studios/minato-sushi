About Minato

Our story, philosophy, and commitment to excellence

Our Story

Minato was born from a passion for authentic Japanese cuisine and a desire to bring the finest sushi experience to our community. Our name, "Minato," means "harbor" in Japanese—a place where traditions meet and flavors converge.

Founded by Chef Kenji Tanaka, who trained for over 15 years in Tokyo, Minato combines traditional techniques with modern creativity. Every piece of sushi we create honors the centuries-old art of Japanese culinary excellence.

We source only the freshest ingredients, prepare our rice with precision, and craft each roll with intention and care.

Our Philosophy

🎯 Quality First

We never compromise on ingredient quality. Every fish, every grain of rice, every vegetable is carefully selected.

🌊 Tradition & Innovation

Respecting traditional methods while embracing creative modern interpretations.

❤️ Crafted with Love

Every dish is prepared with dedication and passion for the art of sushi.
