The Story of Minato

Minato was born from a simple belief: great sushi should be accessible, authentic, and memorable. Our founder, Chef Kenji Tanaka, trained for over 15 years in Tokyo before bringing his craft to your neighborhood.

Every piece of sushi we serve honors traditional techniques while embracing local, seasonal ingredients. We source our fish daily, prepare our rice with precision, and craft each roll with intention.

Minato means "harbor" in Japanese — a place of arrival, connection, and nourishment. We invite you to make our table yours.
